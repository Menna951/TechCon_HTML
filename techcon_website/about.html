The History of TechCon

TechCon started in 2010 with a vision to bring together tech enthusiasts, professionals, and innovators under one roof. What started as a small conference in a local venue has grown into one of the largest tech events worldwide, attracting over 10,000 attendees each year. Over the years, the conference has featured cutting-edge innovations, deep dives into emerging technologies, and networking opportunities for industry leaders.

Our Mission

TechCon is driven by a simple but powerful mission: to create a platform where tech professionals, enthusiasts, and innovators can connect, learn, and shape the future of technology. Our conference brings together the brightest minds in tech to share insights, ideas, and opportunities that can change the world. With every event, we aim to inspire, educate, and empower the next generation of tech leaders.

Notable Past Speakers

Throughout the years, TechCon has featured some of the most prominent figures in the tech industry. From visionary founders to leading researchers, these speakers have shared their knowledge and insights with our attendees. Here are just a few of the influential individuals who have graced the TechCon stage:

Elon Musk

Elon Musk, the CEO of Tesla and SpaceX, spoke at TechCon 2018, inspiring audiences with his vision for the future of electric vehicles, space exploration, and artificial intelligence. His talk on sustainable energy and multi-planetary colonization captured the imagination of all attendees.

Sheryl Sandberg

Sheryl Sandberg, former COO of Facebook, was a keynote speaker at TechCon 2017. She discussed the role of women in tech, leadership strategies, and the importance of diversity and inclusion in the tech industry.

Tim Cook

Tim Cook, the CEO of Apple, delivered a keynote at TechCon 2019, highlighting the future of personal computing, privacy, and Apple's role in shaping the digital landscape. His insights into the intersection of technology and human values resonated deeply with the audience.
